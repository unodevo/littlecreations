What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer by websites that you visit. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

How do we use cookies?

We use cookies for a variety of reasons, including:

  • Authentication: We use cookies to verify your account and remember your login state, allowing you to access our Service without needing to re-enter your login information on each visit.
  • Security: We use cookies to help detect and prevent potential security risks.
  • Preferences: We use cookies to remember your preferences, such as language, currency, or other settings.
  • Analytics: We use cookies to collect anonymous data about how our website is used, helping us improve our Service.
  • Advertising: We use cookies to show you relevant ads, both on our website and on third-party websites.

What types of cookies do we use?

There are two main types of cookies that we use:

  • Session cookies: These are temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. They are used to enable the basic functionality of our website and to maintain your session.
  • Persistent cookies: These are stored on your device between browsing sessions and enable us to remember your preferences or actions across multiple visits.

Third-party cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use various third-party cookies to report usage statistics of the Service, deliver advertisements on and through the Service, and so on.

How can you manage cookies?

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. For more information on how to manage cookies for different browsers, please visit the following links:
